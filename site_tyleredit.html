Hangout Tours

Welcome to Hangout Tours, integrating Google Hangouts, Google Maps, and YOU!

Moving to a new location? Planning out a vacation? Just passionate for discovering new places? Hangout Tours guide you through a location as a group, with input from knowledgeable locals to show you all you need to know, from history, to cuisine, to public transportation. Move through street view with your group, or deviate to explore your own interests. Hangout Tours is all about YOU.

Organize your own hangouts, or check the calendar for upcoming tours.
